Schedule 1 — Services

Project-specific details appended to the Service Agreement.

About Schedule 1: The main agreement sets permanent terms that govern the whole relationship. Schedule 1 is filled in per project or engagement. An email confirming Schedule 1 details is legally sufficient — the full agreement doesn't need to be re-executed each time. Multiple Schedule 1s can run concurrently under one Service Agreement.
